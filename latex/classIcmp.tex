{\footnotesize\ttfamily void Icmp\+::update\+\_\+checksum (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}{ }\end{DoxyParamCaption})\hspace{0.3cm}{\ttfamily [protected]}}



This method updates packet checksum. 

The checksum is the 16-\/bit ones\textquotesingle{}s complement of the one\textquotesingle{}s complement sum of the I\+C\+MP message starting with the I\+C\+MP Type. For computing the checksum , the checksum field should be zero. This checksum may be replaced in the future.
